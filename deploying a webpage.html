My personal background

I grew up in Western Massachusetts and Southern Vermont. When time came around for college, I applied to mostly New England universities and two in New York. Eventually, I ended up at Simmons University on track to be a physical therapist. I graduated with an exercise science degree, but ultimately didn't feel that was the right path for me. I then got my Associate's in Graphic Design at a community college and decided I wanted to expand beyond that, thus leading me to the Harvard Extension School.

My coding background

Fall 2023 was my first semester enrolled at the Extension School and my first class was Fundamentals of Web Development. Prior to this, I had no knowledge at all of any languages (HTML, CSS, Java, etc). At the end of the semester, I was able to incorporate HTML, CSS, and Javascript to make a website. I am far from a pro, but I would say I have a good foundation of the languages. One of my favorite aspects of the final project of creating a website was researching how to make my website unique and learning about how to add the different components. I would say that my skillset in Javascript is the most rusty, but I am mildly comfortable with HTML and CSS. I would love to use the skills I've learned to create single web page applications!
